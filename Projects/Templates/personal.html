Personal Portfolio

Developed an online, personal portfolio to create a space for my own various projects and to showcase my work as an aspiring UI/UX designer. This portfolio totalled approximately two weeks.

The Design Process

For my personal portfolio, I set out to create a communicative platform to showcase my work. Specifically, I wanted to clearly convey my passion for UI/UX design; showcase an interdisplinary set of skills; and prove that said skills were strong and showed further potential.

Accordingly, I believed that the best way to accomplish the above was to implement a portfolio design that, in and of itself, showcased a keen eye for design, clearly depicted my projects, and was simple yet maintained individual character - hopefully I was able to do all of that well!

To lead up to the full implementation, I looked into other designers' portfolios to garner inspiration for design ideation. Through this, I learned of the importance of keeping my portfolio clean, providing well-written case studies accompanied by design process visuals, and higlighting my interdisciplinary skills and experiences.
